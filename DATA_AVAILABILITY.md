# NJIC Ireland — Data Availability & Freedom of Information Matrix
**Version:** 1.0.0 | **Date:** 2026-05-28 | **Jurisdiction:** Ireland

---

## 1. PURPOSE

This matrix catalogs the availability of justice sector data required for the NJIC Integrity Risk Assessment. It distinguishes between:
- **Open Data:** Published proactively online.
- **FOI-Available:** Obtainable via Freedom of Information request (FOI Act 2014).
- **Restricted:** Subject to statutory exemptions (personal data, ongoing investigations, national security).
- **Not Available:** Not collected or not disclosed by the agency.

## 3. FOI REQUEST TEMPLATES

### Template A: General Integrity Metrics

```
To: FOI Officer, [Agency Name]
Date: [Date]
Re: Freedom of Information Request — Justice Integrity Metrics
I request the following information under the Freedom of Information Act 2014:
The total number of [misconduct complaints / disciplinary proceedings / procurement irregularities]
recorded in [Year], aggregated by category and outcome.
The number of Freedom of Information requests received and the percentage granted in full
during [Year].
The average time (in days) to publish the agency's annual report following the end of the
financial year, for the period [Start] to [End].
Any internal audit reports on [corruption risks / procurement / HR integrity] completed
in [Year], redacted to remove personal data.
I do not require personal data or information that would identify any individual.
I am willing to accept aggregated, anonymized data.
Preferred format: CSV or Excel.
Yours sincerely,
[Name]
[Contact]
```
